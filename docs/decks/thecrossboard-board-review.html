CrossBoard + Abiding PracticeYouTube
Public channel strategy

Use YouTube to carry the public witness

Pros

  • Search, recommendations, and public credibility.
  • Easy clips, premieres, livestreams, and embeds.
  • Strong top-of-funnel for films, conversations, and teachings.

Cons

  • Not a reliable paid-access layer.
  • Comments and community fragment off-platform.
  • Attention can stay on YouTube instead of converting.
CrossBoard + Abiding PracticeHosted Communities
Skool / Circle / Mighty

Fast to start, weak as Abiding Practice’s identity layer

Pros

  • Payments, events, community spaces, and calls are packaged.
  • Useful if we need a short-term paid community pilot.
  • Low engineering cost at the beginning.

Cons

  • Member identity, data, and engagement live outside CrossBoard.
  • Weak fit for artists’ portfolios, partner profiles, and sponsored access.
  • Harder to connect formation events to creative opportunity.
CrossBoard + Abiding PracticeCrossBoard Option
Owned platform

More control, less generic community drift

Pros

  • Owns the relationship, graph, paywall, and archive.
  • Connects Abiding media to artists, teams, partners, and events.
  • Supports spiritual formation and creative opportunity together.

Cons

  • Requires entitlement and admin work before paid access.
  • Live sessions still need Zoom, Cloudflare, or LiveKit.
  • Needs disciplined scope to avoid overbuilding.

CrossBoard + Abiding PracticeRisks
What we must manage

The risk is not choosing CrossBoard. It is scattering the movement.

Fragmentation

Audience without ownership

Keep YouTube public; bring members back to CrossBoard.

Overbuild

Live rooms too early

Use Zoom first; add LiveKit after demand is clear.

Weak gates

Paid value leaks

Use entitlements and signed replay instead of unlisted links.

CrossBoard + Abiding PracticeApproval Ask
For board review

Approve CrossBoard as the Abiding Practice platform layer

Proceed with the owned platform approach while continuing to use YouTube, Zoom, and Cloudflare where they are strongest.

Decision package

  • Approve CrossBoard as the canonical home for members and events.
  • Approve MVP entitlements, paid access, and organization tiers.
  • Approve YouTube public distribution and Zoom pilot sessions.
  • Approve Cloudflare gated replays; defer LiveKit until needed.
